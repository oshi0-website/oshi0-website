Children's Ministry


Sunday School

Every Sunday from 9:37-10:27, Our Savior's children are welcome to join us at Sunday School. During Sunday School, we spend time learning about the Bible stories we have heard during worship. Through games, storytime, crafts, and other activities, children engage in faith forming activities that connect them to the love of Jesus.


Nursery

During worship, Our Savior's littlest ones (babies-kindergarten) are welcome to our nursery off of the narthex. Staffed by volunteers, the nursery is equipped with age appropriate toys, books, and activities. Please be sure to sign your child in and out. You can also find our changing table here. There are Sundays when the nursery is not staffed (we don't always have enough volunteers). You are welcome to stay in the nursery with your little one and watch the service on the monitor. You are also welcome to volunteer for the nursery team so we can keep the nursery open every Sunday.


Worship

No one is too young to join us in worship. During each service, children are welcome to come to the front and participate in a children's message. In the back of the church, children's activities and crayons are also available for your child during the service. We welcome all children to our services and are happy to support you in whatever way we can to make worship a special time to be gathered as family.


Vacation Bible eXperience

Each summer, children (PK-4th grade) gather for a week of Vacation Bible eXperience at Our Savior. During this week, children engage in Bible stories, music, games, and activities as they discover in exciting ways the great love Jesus as for them. VBX usually takes place right after school gets out in June. Keep an eye on the calendar for the dates!



Junior High

Here is our schedule for Our Savior's Junior High activities!


Truth Tuesdays

We gather from 6:00 - 7:30PM on the 2nd and 4th Tuesday of each month for food, games, fun with friends, and interactive Bible Experiences.

Fun Fridays

Once a quarter, we meet on a Friday night for fun in and out of the youth room.

Half-Day Hullabaloo

On select Minimum Days, we get together for crazy fun when school lets out.

Service Saturdays

Once a semester, we join forces with the Senior High to serve our community.
