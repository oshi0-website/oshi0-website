FELLOWSHIP

Around Memorial Day and Labor Day, we head to the beach for a day of fun, fellowship, and some great BBQ. Keep an eye out for the next one!



Every other year or so, we rent some double decker buses and Spaghetti Factory and head into Honolulu to see the Christmas lights.



And don't forget all the choirs (Concordia Bronxville, Concordia Irvine, Concordia Portland, St. Peter's, Brisbane, Australia), Rob the Donut Man, Skit Guys and others. God is good and we like to celebrate His goodness. Watch the latest announcements to find out where we're going next! And if you have some ideas - we'd love to hear them!
